Nutrient-Packed Smoothies & Wellness Shots

Discover delicious, customizable smoothies and energizing wellness shots to boost your health and energy levels.

Today's Smoothies

    Smoothie Integration

    Incorporate 2–3 smoothies daily as "extras" between meals or alongside breakfast to boost nutrient intake.

    Energy

    Natural sugars and complex carbs provide sustained energy throughout the day.

    Nutrition

    Pack multiple servings of fruits and vegetables into one delicious drink.

    Lactation Support

    Ingredients like oats, flaxseed, and almonds can support milk production.

    Basic Smoothie Formula

    • Base: 1 cup unsweetened almond or oat milk
    • Frozen fruits: banana, berries, mango (provides fiber and antioxidants)
    • Leafy greens: spinach or kale (iron, calcium)
    • Protein: Greek yogurt, protein powder, oats, or silken tofu
    • Healthy fats: avocado, nut butter, ground flaxseed or chia seeds

    Energy Boost

    Triple Berry Oat Smoothie

    320 cal 5 min 3 fruits

    Ingredients:

    • 1/3 cup frozen blueberries
    • 1/3 cup frozen blackberries
    • 1/3 cup frozen strawberries
    • 1/4 cup rolled oats
    • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed
    • 1 cup oat milk
    • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Combine all ingredients in blender
    2. Blend until smooth
    3. Add more oat milk if too thick

    Supports milk supply and provides sustained energy.

    Green & Lactation Support

    Green Lactation Smoothie

    280 cal 5 min 2 veg

    Ingredients:

    • 1 cup spinach
    • 1 ripe banana
    • 1/2 cup frozen peach slices
    • 1/4 avocado
    • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
    • 1 cup oat milk
    • 1 tbsp ground flaxseed

    Instructions:

    1. Add all ingredients to blender
    2. Blend until smooth
    3. Add water to adjust thickness if needed

    Rich in fiber, healthy fats, and galactagogues like oat milk and flaxseed.

    Pro Smoothie Tips

    Perfect Texture

    Use frozen fruits instead of ice to create thick, creamy smoothies without diluting flavor.

    Hidden Veggies

    Add mild vegetables like spinach or cauliflower—you'll get the nutrition without affecting taste.

    Prep Ahead

    Create smoothie packs by portioning ingredients into freezer bags for quick morning blending.

    Balance Flavors

    Combine sweet fruits with tart ones like berries or citrus for a balanced flavor profile.

    Wellness Shots

    Quick, concentrated drinks for immunity, digestion, and energy. Take as a "shot" in the morning or between meals.

    Quick Boost

    Fast-acting nutrients and antioxidants in a small dose.

    Immunity

    Ginger, turmeric, and citrus help support your immune system.

    Immune Support Shots

    Ginger-Lemon Shot

    15 cal 2 min 1 shot

    Ingredients:

    • 1 inch fresh ginger root
    • 1/2 lemon, juiced
    • 1 tsp honey (optional)
    • Pinch of cayenne (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Grate or juice ginger.
    2. Mix with lemon juice, honey, and cayenne.
    3. Strain and serve as a shot.

    Anti-inflammatory and immune-boosting.

    Turmeric-Orange Shot

    20 cal 2 min 1 shot

    Ingredients:

    • 1/2 orange, juiced
    • 1/2 inch fresh turmeric root (or 1/4 tsp powder)
    • Pinch of black pepper
    • 1/2 tsp honey

    Instructions:

    1. Juice orange and turmeric.
    2. Mix with pepper and honey.
    3. Strain and serve as a shot.

    Anti-inflammatory and antioxidant-rich.

    Digestion Support Shots

    Apple Cider Vinegar Shot

    10 cal 1 min 1 shot

    Ingredients:

    • 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
    • 2 tbsp water
    • 1 tsp honey
    • Pinch of cinnamon (optional)

    Instructions:

    1. Mix all ingredients well.
    2. Drink as a shot before meals.

    Supports digestion and blood sugar balance.
